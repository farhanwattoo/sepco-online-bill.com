SEPCO Online Bill

Check and understand your Sukkur Electric Power Company (SEPCO) bill in one place: validate your 14 digit reference number, estimate charges, and print a clean duplicate-bill summary.

SEPCO supplies electricity to upper Sindh, including Sukkur, Rohri, Khairpur, Larkana, Shikarpur, Jacobabad, Ghotki, Kandhkot, Kashmore, and Dadu. Every SEPCO bill carries a 14 digit reference number, and you need that number for almost every billing task: checking the latest bill, downloading a duplicate copy, or confirming a payment.

The helper at the top of this page validates the reference number format, estimates your total from units, the per-unit rate, and taxes, and builds a printable summary card. It works entirely in your browser and does not fetch live billing data — for the officially billed amount, always use official SEPCO channels.

The guides on this site cover the questions that usually follow: where to find the reference number, how to download and print a duplicate bill, what the charges, taxes, and units on the bill mean, when the due date applies, and what to do if a bill never arrives.

Frequently Asked Questions

These questions cover the real follow-up tasks users usually have after opening a bill helper, including reference numbers, bill understanding, print use, mobile access, and what this page can and cannot do.

Does this fetch live SEPCO bill data?

No. This browser tool validates the format and builds a local estimate only.

What number do I need?

You normally need the 14 digit reference number printed on an older bill.

How do I check my SEPCO bill online?

Take the 14 digit reference number printed on any previous SEPCO bill and enter it in an official SEPCO bill lookup to see the live billed amount. You can also use the helper on this page to validate the number format and prepare a printable estimate first.

How do I use the SEPCO Online Bill helper?

Enter the reference number first, then add units and charges if you want a local estimate before generating the printable summary.

Is this SEPCO Online Bill page the official live bill server?

No. This page is a local helper for format checks, estimate workflows, and quick duplicate-style summaries.

Where is the reference number printed on a SEPCO bill?

The reference number appears in the top section of every printed SEPCO bill, usually near your name and address block. Our reference number guide shows exactly where to look on the bill layout.

Which areas does SEPCO cover?

Sukkur Electric Power Company serves upper Sindh, including Sukkur, Rohri, Khairpur, Larkana, Shikarpur, Jacobabad, Ghotki, Kandhkot, Kashmore, and Dadu.

What should I do if my SEPCO bill never arrived?

You can still pay on time by preparing a duplicate bill with your reference number. The bill-not-received guide on this site walks through the exact steps to get a copy before the due date.
