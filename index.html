Frequently asked questions

How do I check my actual SEPCO bill?

Enter your 14-digit reference number at the official portal, bill.pitc.com.pk/sepcobill. It’s free with no registration. Step-by-step guide.

Is this the official SEPCO website?

No — this is an independent guide. We explain the process, maintain current tariff tables, and provide a bill estimator, but only the PITC portal shows your real bill. See our disclaimer.

What are the electricity rates right now?

FY 2025-26 domestic rates range from Rs 3.95/unit (lifeline) to Rs 47.69/unit (above 700 units), plus surcharges and taxes. Full slab tables.

Why is my bill suddenly so high?

The usual suspects: losing protected status by crossing 200 units, a positive fuel price adjustment, or an estimated meter reading. Diagnosis checklist.

Which areas does SEPCO serve?

Upper Sindh, including Sukkur, Ghotki, Khairpur, Shikarpur, Jacobabad, Kashmore, Larkana, Qambar Shahdadkot, Naushahro Feroze and Dadu districts.

Can I pay my bill from my phone?

Yes — JazzCash, Easypaisa and every major banking app pay SEPCO bills by reference number, free. Two-minute walkthrough.
